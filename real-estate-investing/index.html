Our Investment Strategies

🏠 Long-Term Rentals

The backbone of most Utah investor portfolios. We'll help you find single-family homes and small multifamily in high-demand rental markets, then position them for stable monthly cash flow, long-term appreciation, and the tax advantages that come with buy-and-hold investing.

✓ Consistent monthly cash flow
✓ Long-term tenant stability
✓ Mortgage paydown & appreciation

🏖️ Short-Term Rentals (Airbnb)

Higher income potential in tourism-driven markets. We'll help you identify neighborhoods where short-term rentals outperform long-term leases, navigate local regulations, and run the numbers on nightly rates, occupancy, and management costs before you buy.

✓ Higher revenue per night
✓ Flexibility for personal use
✓ Tourism-driven markets

🔄 BRRRR Method

Buy, Rehab, Rent, Refinance, Repeat — the strategy experienced investors use to scale quickly by recycling the same pool of capital. We'll help you source undervalued properties, plan renovations that force appreciation, and refinance out at a higher valuation so you can roll into the next deal.

✓ Forced appreciation
✓ Recycle your capital
✓ Build equity quickly

🏢 Multifamily (2-4 Units)

More units under one roof means better cash flow, built-in risk diversification, and easier management. We'll help you find duplexes, triplexes, and fourplexes that qualify for owner-occupant financing — the same loans available to first-time buyers — so you can start investing with as little as 3.5% down.

✓ Higher cash-on-cash returns
✓ Risk diversification
✓ Economies of scale

OUR FAVORITE STRATEGY

House Hacking

House hacking is one of the best ways to get started in real estate investing. Buy a multi-unit property, live in one unit, and rent out the others — your tenants help cover your mortgage while you build equity and wealth.

✓ Live for free or at a reduced cost
✓ Qualify with FHA loans (as low as 3.5% down)
✓ Build equity while gaining landlord experience
✓ Scale into your next property faster
