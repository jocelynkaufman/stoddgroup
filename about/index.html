About Stodd Group

A team of Utah realtors and active investors helping first-time buyers and new investors build wealth through real estate.

The Turning Point

From Teaching to Financial Freedom

Stodd Group was born from a simple idea: the best teachers are the ones who've walked the path themselves. Our founder, Jocelyn Stoddard, spent four years as a high school teacher while quietly building a rental portfolio on the side. The day she retired from teaching — with dozens of Utah properties paying her mortgage — it became clear this strategy shouldn't stay a secret.

Today, Stodd Group helps first-time buyers and new investors across Utah follow the same playbook: buy your first house hack, let tenants build your equity, repeat. We've done it ourselves, and we're here to help you do it too.

Jocelyn Stoddard

Founder & Realtor

Jocelyn went from teaching high school to retiring on rental income in just four years — all while building a Utah portfolio one house hack at a time. Troy Owen

Realtor

Troy is the team's first-time buyer specialist, focused on making sure new homeowners land in the right house — not just any house. Hardworking and dedicated, he treats every client's purchase like his own, putting in the time and legwork to find the property that fits their life and goals. Whether you're buying your first home or your first investment, Troy will be in your corner from first showing to closing day.

Our Brokerage

Proudly Part of Canovo Group

Stodd Group operates under Canovo Group, a Utah-based real estate brokerage with more than 20 years of experience and over $450 million in transactions across residential, multifamily, and commercial deals.

Their investment-first approach — deal screening, underwriting, and structuring focused on cash flow, appreciation, and tax efficiency — aligns directly with how we serve our clients. Together we give Utah buyers and investors the backing of a seasoned brokerage with the personal, education-driven approach Stodd Group is known for.
