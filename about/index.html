Where It All Started

From Small-Town Athlete to World Traveler

I was born in the small town of Tooele, Utah. I played softball since I was 5 and my whole life was consumed by the sport throughout my childhood. I have always tried to dedicate myself as much as possible to whatever I am doing at that moment.

Eventually, that dedication provided me with an opportunity to play softball in college. I attended the University of Providence where I double majored in Health and Human Performance and Health and Physical Education.

After graduating with my bachelor's degree, softball once again gave me an amazing opportunity. In the summer of 2017, I played professional softball in Sweden. I was immersed in another culture for a few months and had the chance to experience Europe firsthand.

The Turning Point

From Teaching to Financial Freedom

Once I got home from Sweden it was time to find a job and start making a living for myself. I got a job at a high school in Utah as a health teacher and the head softball coach.

After 4 years of hard work and dedication to creating financial freedom for myself, I was able to retire from teaching. Now I have dozens of rentals and have created a life of financial freedom for myself.

What I Do Now

Helping Others Build Wealth

My main focus now is helping to educate others on how to create financial freedom while I continue to grow my real estate empire.
