Privacy Policy

How Stodd Group Real Estate collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Stodd Group Real Estate ("Stodd Group," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit our website at jocelynkaufman.github.io/stoddgroup (the "Site"), contact us, or engage our real estate services. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information collected automatically as you use the Site, and information from third parties.

Information you provide: When you submit a contact form, book a strategy call, subscribe to our newsletter, request a property valuation, or communicate with us, you may give us your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, property details, budget, timeline, and other details relevant to a real estate transaction.

Information collected automatically: When you visit the Site, our servers and third-party analytics tools may log your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, time spent, and similar usage data. We may use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to remember preferences and measure how the Site is used.

Information from third parties: We may receive information from real estate platforms (including MLS services), lead-generation partners, social media platforms, and service providers who help us operate our business.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide the real estate services you request;
  • Match you with suitable properties, financing resources, or investment opportunities;
  • Send you newsletters, educational content, market updates, and promotional messages (you can unsubscribe at any time);
  • Operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Site and our services;
  • Analyze usage trends and measure the effectiveness of our content and marketing;
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, and real estate licensing obligations; and
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, or illegal activity.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers that host our Site, send email, host forms, process analytics, or otherwise support our business;
  • Real estate partners — such as lenders, title companies, inspectors, contractors, and co-operating brokers — when needed to facilitate a transaction you have requested;
  • Brokerage and MLS entities as required by our real estate license and local MLS rules;
  • Legal and regulatory authorities when we believe disclosure is required by law, subpoena, or court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Stodd Group, our clients, or others; and
  • Successors in the event of a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We and our analytics providers use cookies and similar technologies to recognize your browser, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors interact with the Site. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect Site functionality.

Third-Party Links

The Site may link to third-party websites, tools, or services (for example, Amazon product pages, MLS listings, or scheduling tools). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing information with them.

Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as needed to provide our services, comply with our legal and regulatory obligations (including real estate record-keeping requirements), resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Choices and Rights

You may:

  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in any message we send;
  • Ask us to access, correct, update, or delete personal information we hold about you, subject to legal exceptions; and
  • Opt out of certain analytics cookies through your browser settings or the tools those providers offer.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under state or national privacy laws. To exercise any right, contact us at the address below. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information we collect. No internet transmission or storage system is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be highlighted on the Site or communicated to you directly where appropriate.

Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices? Reach us through our contact page and we will respond as promptly as we can.
