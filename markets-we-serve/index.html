Young demographics, a strong job market, and relatively affordable prices make it a standout.

FAMILY FAVORITE

Davis County

Situated between Salt Lake City and Ogden, Davis County is known for its family-friendly communities, excellent schools, and convenient access to both metro areas. Cities like Bountiful, Farmington, and Layton offer a suburban feel with easy commutes and strong community values. A great market for rental properties and first-time buyers.

Weber County

Weber County, anchored by Ogden, is an investor's dream. With lower entry prices compared to Salt Lake County, strong rental yields, and a rapidly revitalizing downtown Ogden scene, this market offers excellent cash flow opportunities. Outdoor recreation, a growing arts community, and FrontRunner access make it increasingly attractive.
