The heart of Utah's real estate market

FEATURED MARKET

Salt Lake County

The epicenter of Utah's booming economy, Salt Lake County offers everything from urban condos in downtown SLC to suburban family homes in Sandy and Draper. With a thriving tech sector, world-class outdoor recreation, and strong population growth, this market delivers consistent appreciation and rental demand.

Utah County

Utah County is one of the fastest-growing regions in the nation. Anchored by Provo, Orem, and the booming Silicon Slopes corridor in Lehi, this market offers incredible opportunities for both homebuyers and investors. Young demographics, a strong job market, and relatively affordable prices make it a standout.

Davis County

FAMILY FAVORITE

Davis County

Situated between Salt Lake City and Ogden, Davis County is known for its family-friendly communities, excellent schools, and convenient access to both metro areas. Cities like Bountiful, Farmington, and Layton offer a suburban feel with easy commutes and strong community values. A great market for rental properties and first-time buyers.

UNDERVALUED GEM

Weber County

Weber County, anchored by Ogden, is an investor's dream. With lower entry prices compared to Salt Lake County, strong rental yields, and a rapidly revitalizing downtown Ogden scene, this market offers excellent cash flow opportunities. Outdoor recreation, a growing arts community, and FrontRunner access make it increasingly attractive.

LUXURY RESORT MARKET

Summit County

Anchored by Park City and the world-class skiing of Deer Valley and Park City Mountain Resort, Summit County is Utah's premier luxury and short-term rental market. Limited inventory, strong second-home demand, and year-round resort appeal make it a unique long-hold play — high entry prices, but exceptional appreciation history and STR income potential.

EMERGING MOUNTAIN VALLEY

Wasatch County

Heber Valley is one of Utah's fastest-growing mountain communities — a quieter, more affordable alternative to Park City just 20 minutes away. With Deer Creek Reservoir, Jordanelle, and the new Mayflower / Deer Valley East Village expansion driving demand, Wasatch County offers a rare mix of small-town charm, mountain access, and strong long-term appreciation potential.
